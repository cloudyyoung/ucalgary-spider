Greek and Roman Studies GRST
GRST 205 209 211 305 306 309 311 313 315 317 321 323 325 327 335 337 339 341 345 347 355 357 413 415 417 419 421 423 425 431 433 445 447 455 457 459 483 491 494 499 501 504 525 551 601 603 651
 

For more information about these courses, see the Department of Classics and Religion website: arts.ucalgary.ca/classics-religion/classics-religion.

For courses in the ancient Greek and Latin languages see listings under Greek and Latin.

The research topics courses Greek and Roman Studies 413-457 (except Greek and Roman Studies 431) do not have specific prerequisites, but students are strongly advised to have taken at least two 300-level Greek and Roman Studies courses with grades of at least "C-" before enrolling in them.
