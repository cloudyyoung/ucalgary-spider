Senior Courses
 
Professional Land Management 475       Introduction to Professional Land Management
Professional land management including both technical and economic considerations in the search for and development and marketing of oil and gas resources. A field trip and/or simulation exercise may be required.
Course Hours: 3 units; (3-1.5T)
Prerequisite(s): Admission to the Haskayne School of Business and 54 units.
back to top
Professional Land Management 573       Energy Business Agreements
Legal requirements in the acquisition, disposition, and exploration of and production from energy rights and contracts in Canada.
Course Hours: 3 units; (3-0)
Prerequisite(s): Admission to the Haskayne School of Business and Professional Land Management 475.
back to top
Professional Land Management 579       Professional Land Management Policy
Prepare graduates to effectively contribute to the management of energy properties throughout the life of the properties by increasing the understanding and awareness of corporate resource property valuation techniques.
Course Hours: 3 units; (3-0)
Prerequisite(s): Admission to the Haskayne School of Business, Professional Land Management 475, 573, and Finance 317.
Corequisite(s): or Prerequisite: Professional Land Management 587.
back to top
Professional Land Management 583       Management of Energy Regulation
Overview of key regulations and surface regulatory principles important to energy resources.
Course Hours: 3 units; (3-0)
Prerequisite(s): Admission to the Haskayne School of Business and 54 units.
back to top
Professional Land Management 587       Cases in Professional Land Management
A "Hands on" case approach to the typical situations and decision-making processes faced by landmen and commercial negotiators. Historical perspectives, contemporary tactics, and modern applications for departments and energy companies.
Course Hours: 3 units; (3-0)
Prerequisite(s): Admission to the Haskayne School of Business, Professional Land Management 475, 573 and Finance 317.
